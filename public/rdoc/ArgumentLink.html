normalize_uri() click to toggle source

Normalizes the link i.e., adds protocol to the URL if it is not already present Example:- 

  'http://www.google.com/' gives 'http://www.google.com/'
  'www.google.com/' gives 'http://www.google.com/'

  def normalize_uri
